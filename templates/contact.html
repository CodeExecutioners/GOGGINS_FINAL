Dave & Karen Goggin

Mission Statement

The purpose of Goggin Ballroom Dancing is to provide people of all ages and backgrounds an affordable opportunity to learn and maintain the skills necessary when dancing in a variety of social occasions.

Biography

Dave and Karen Goggin have over 45 years of combined professional teaching experience in Ballroom, Latin, and Swing dancing. They teach a combination of private and group lessons in the Chippewa Valley and Central Wisconsin including Eau Claire, Marshfield, Menomonie and Rice Lake. In addition, weekly and monthly practice dances are offered to all levels of social dancers. Over the past three decades they have presented their skills to a variety of organizations: UWEC Viennese Ball and student organization "Two To Tango", UW Wood, and Barron County Continuing Education, Chippewa Valley Dance Club and various public schools and nursing homes throughout the area.
